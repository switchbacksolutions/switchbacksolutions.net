## Key Achievements

### Mission-Driven Design

Designed a beautiful, clean website that effectively communicates Sol Village's three core values: affordability, community, and sustainability. The design reflects the warmth and intentionality of the project while maintaining clarity and accessibility.

### Marketing Automation

Implemented marketing automation to manage the interest list signup process, nurturing potential residents, investors, and partners. The system helps Sol Village build their founding circle while maintaining engagement with their community.

### Community-Focused Content

Crafted content that speaks to the unique needs of Nevada County residents—teachers, firefighters, farmers, and artists—while clearly articulating the benefits of tiny home cohousing and community-centered living.

## Results

- Beautiful, mission-aligned website design
- Automated interest list management
- Clear communication of affordability, community, and sustainability values
- Effective lead nurturing for future residents and investors
